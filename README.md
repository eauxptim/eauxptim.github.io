# Eauxptim

Eauxptim SRL develops research, software, and engineering solutions for water and wastewater process optimization.

## Overview

Eauxptim focuses on advanced digital and technical solutions for the water sector, including:

- water and wastewater treatment optimization
- software and SaaS tools
- digital twins and process models
- engineering and technical studies
- AI-enabled and data-driven decision support
- research and innovation collaboration
